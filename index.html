Italia

Benvenuti tutti! My name is Diana and I want to tell you about one of my favorite countries to visit, Italy!

Italy is a country full of beautiful scenery, delicious food, incredible history and loving and warm and inviting people. What's not to like?!

Where to Go

There is so many different cities to visit. Each one so very different. There is a perfect place for everyone. My personal favorite, Florence. I love the combination of old with the new. It amazes me how Florence has been able to maintain so much of their history of their art and culture while still be a major city. Just because Florence is my favorite city and I would drop it all for a chance to move there tonight, doesn't mean it will be your favorite.
